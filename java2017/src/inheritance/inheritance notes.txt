Inheritance in real life is deriving something specific from something generic

ex: santro is a specific instance of generic category Car

inheritance helps in the reuse of code.

There exists a "is - a - kind -of " relationship among classes in inheritance.

ex: manager is a kind of Employee.

in Aggregation ,  we can make up objects from other objects.

Ex:Engine is a part of car.

in aggregation we have " is a part of "  relationship among classes.

Types of inheritance
1)Single 
2)multilevel
3)multiple
